About Lily Hong Linh Ha

Lily Hong Linh Ha is a dedicated individual who transitioned from a background in International Business and Trade to pursue a career in Clinical Psychology. This shift was prompted by her realization during the COVID pandemic about the increasing mental health issues faced by her friends and colleagues. She recognized that her previous job was not fulfilling her desire to impact people's lives positively.

Since childhood, Lily dreamed of opening her own private psychology consultation clinic. To make that dream a reality, she decided to pursue her Master’s degree in Australia, where she found that the psychology field is more established and better trained than in her home country of Vietnam.
