About Me

I am a psychology learner currently transitioning from a business background toward the field of psychology. Over time, I became increasingly interested in understanding human emotions, behavior, and the ways individuals cope with psychological challenges.

Through independent study and introductory psychology courses, I began exploring topics such as emotional regulation, self-awareness, and psychological well-being. These experiences gradually shaped my decision to pursue further academic training in psychology.

I am particularly interested in adult psychotherapy and the role of psychological support in helping individuals navigate emotional difficulties and personal growth.

This portfolio documents my learning journey, volunteer interests, and personal reflections as I prepare for graduate-level study in psychology.
